Welcome to GitHub Pages.

TIDAQ stands for Tissue IDentification and Quantification tool. It can be applied to quantify lower leg PQCT, mid-thigh CT images. It identifies soft and hard tissues and computes area and density measures. The main goal is epidemiological studies of aging and metabolic diseases.
